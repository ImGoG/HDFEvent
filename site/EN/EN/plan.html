ACCESSIBILITY :
Lille Grand Palais offers a big accessibility and an integrated parking of 1186 parking spots as well as more than 7000 spots located at 10 minutes walking.
In train : Lille Europe TGV (French high-speed train) station and Lille Flandres station at 9 minutes walking.
In car : At the highway exists A1 and A25.
In plane : 3 international airports: Lille Lesquin at 15 minutes driving, Paris-Charles de Gaulle at 60 minutes driving and Bruxelles Zaventem at 70 minutes driving.
In bike : "Zénith" station in front of Lille Grand Palais and 2 V'Lille station (4 minutes walking): Cité administrative and Hôtel de région.
In subway : 4 stations : Lille Grand Palais (5 minutes walking), Mairie de Lille (7 minutes walking) and Gare Lille Flandres and Lille Europe (9 minutes walking).
In bus : Citadine de Lille :Line 90 stop "Lille-Zénith", Line 18 : stop « Lille Grand Palais ».
